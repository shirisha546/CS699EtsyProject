How Loyalty Points are Earned:

Customers earn 10 loyalty points for every purchase exceeding $500. You can redeem the loyalty points to make purchases. Every 10 loyalty points are equivalent to $5. To determine the loyalty level, we assess the customer's all-time points. If their accumulated points fall below 100, they're categorized as Level 0. Advancing to Level 1 requires accumulating between 100 and 199 points, while Level 2 is attained with points ranging from 200 to 249. Beyond this, exceeding 250 points elevates the customer to the prestigious status of "Gold Customer". Adjustments to these thresholds or labels can be made to better align with specific program goals and customer incentives.
